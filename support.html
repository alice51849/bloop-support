Bloop Support

Quick answers for your shared pet, widget, pairing, and purchase.

How do we raise the same Bloop?

One person creates a Bloop and shares the 12-character pair code. The other person opens Bloop, enters that code, and joins the existing pet. Everyone joining the same code sees and cares for the same Bloop.

How do I add the interactive widget?

Touch and hold an empty area of the Home Screen, tap Edit, tap Add Widget, search for Bloop, choose a size, and add it. The medium widget includes feed, play, and cuddle actions. The small widget includes quick feeding.

Why did the widget not update immediately?

Confirm that both devices are signed in to iCloud and have an internet connection. Open Bloop once after installation so iOS can register background updates. iOS may delay silent updates in Low Power Mode, and it does not guarantee background delivery after a user force-quits an app.

How do I enable reminders?

Open Settings inside Bloop and turn on Care Reminders and Partner Updates. You can change notification permission later in the iPhone Settings app.

How does Bloop Pro work?

Bloop Pro is one non-consumable purchase. It permanently unlocks all seven hand-drawn characters and lets you switch anytime. Sharing stays available to everyone. There is no subscription. Use Restore Purchase in the Bloop Pro screen when moving to another device with the same Apple Account.

Can my Bloop die?

No. Low stats only make Bloop hungry, sleepy, or in need of affection. Bloop is designed to feel comforting rather than punishing.

How do I delete the shared pet?

Open Bloop Settings, select Shared Pet Data, and choose Delete Shared Pet. This deletes the encrypted iCloud record and clears the pet, messages, and care history for everyone sharing it.
