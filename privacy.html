Privacy Policy

Effective July 10, 2026

1. Summary

Bloop has no advertising, tracking, third-party analytics SDKs, or developer account system. Shared-pet content is encrypted on your device before it is stored using Apple's iCloud service.

2. Information you choose to create

Bloop lets you enter display names, a pet name, a short shared message, and care activity. This content is needed to provide the shared-pet experience. Bloop encrypts the shared pet record before upload. The secret pair code is not uploaded in readable form.

3. iCloud and synchronization

Bloop uses Apple CloudKit to synchronize the encrypted shared-pet record between paired devices. Apple may process technical service information under Apple's own privacy policy. The developer does not operate a separate server and cannot read the encrypted pet content without the pair code.

4. Local device data

Your app preferences, purchase entitlement, device participant identifier, pet cache, and pairing information are stored locally in the Bloop App Group so the app and widget can work together.

5. Notifications

If you enable reminders, Bloop schedules local care reminders. CloudKit may send silent Apple Push Notification service messages so your widget can refresh after another person cares for the pet. Bloop does not use notifications for advertising.

6. Purchases

Bloop Pro is a one-time, non-consumable In-App Purchase handled by Apple StoreKit. We do not receive or store your payment card information. Apple may provide purchase entitlement information needed to unlock Pro.

7. Data we do not collect

We do not collect advertising identifiers, precise location, contacts, photos, microphone recordings, browsing history, or health data. Bloop contains no third-party advertising, tracking, or analytics tools.

8. Deleting shared data

Open Bloop, go to Settings, choose Shared Pet Data, and tap Delete Shared Pet. This removes the encrypted shared record from iCloud and clears the local pet, messages, and care history. Removing the app also removes its local data, subject to normal Apple device and backup behavior.

9. Children

Bloop is a general-audience app and is not directed specifically at children. We do not knowingly collect readable personal information from children or anyone else.

10. Security

Bloop uses a long random pair code, a hashed CloudKit record identifier, and AES-GCM authenticated encryption for shared content. No method of storage is absolutely risk-free, so keep your pair code private and share it only with people you trust.

11. Changes and contact

If this policy changes, this page and its effective date will be updated. Questions can be sent to alice51849@hotmail.com.
